Player Shortcut Keys

The following shortcut keys are available in Mediasite Player. Some keyboard shortcuts are not available in all layouts and depend on the features exposed in the Player. These shortcuts are indicated with an asterisk (*):

Shortcut Keys

Command

Alt+F1

Open the Player Help Menu.

Space bar

Play or pause the presentation.

L

View the presentation's links.*

Ctrl+Up Arrow
(Ctrl+ ↑)

Volume up.

Ctrl+Down Arrow
(Ctrl+↓)

Volume down.

M

Mute the presentation's audio.

Left Arrow (←)

Skip back 3 seconds in the presentation.

Right Arrow (→)

Skip forward 3 seconds in the presentation.

Ctrl+Left Arrow
(Ctrl+ ←)

Go to the previous slide.

Ctrl+Right Arrow
(Ctrl + →)

Go to the next slide.
