Viewing Presentations on a Mac

Presentations will only play on Macs using OS X 10.4.8 or later.

Browser Requirements on a Mac

The following browsers will play Mediasite presentations on a Mac:

  • Safari 2.0.4 or later (3.0.4 or later recommended)
  • Firefox 2.0 or later

Download the Silverlight plug-in

The Silverlight plug-in allows you to view presentations on the Mac.

Click the Get Microsoft Silverlight logo in the video screen to download and install the plug-in.

Still not working?

Contact your Mediasite administrator to make sure that HTTP streaming is enabled on the media server on port 80.
