Display Options

Adjust the positions of the Player's video and image windows using the display option controls.

View full screen

Enter
full screen

Click Enter Full Screen to expand the presentation so that it fills the entire screen. Press Esc to exit full-screen mode.

Note: When you click Enter Full Screen in the the Silverlight Video Only player, only the video is displayed.

View picture in picture

Picture
in Picture

Click Picture in Picture to display a smaller image or video window in the right corner of the larger video or image window, respectively. Click Swap Media Elements to alternate windows.

Top

View side by side

Side by side

Click Side by Side to display a smaller image or video window next to the larger video or image window, respectively. Click Swap Media Elements to alternate windows.

Top

View full frame

Full Frame

Click Full Frame to view an enlarged video or image window. Click Swap Media Elements to switch the window displayed.

Top

Pop out (embeddable players only)

Pop Out

Click Pop Out when viewing an embedded presentation to launch a second browser window with the presentation displayed in full-screen mode.

Top

Switch windows

Swap
Media
Elements

Click Swap Media Elements to switch the position of the video and image windows or alternate between the two when using any of the display options.

Note: When viewing a presentation in the Small Silverlight Player, you will have to click Enter Full Screen, then click Swap Media Elements, and finally click Full Frame to arrange the windows.
