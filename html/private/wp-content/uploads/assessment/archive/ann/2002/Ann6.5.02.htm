Announcements - June 5, 2002

ARPT vote: The results of the recent OCOB ballot to change the college's "Statement of Policies, Criteria, and Procedures for Academic Appointment, Reappointment, Tenure, and Promotion" are as follows: yes = 22; no = 3. The revised document will be forwarded to the Provost for his action.

Strategic Planning: Work products from the six strategic planning task forces will be posted on http://ocob-faculty.calpoly.edu/ on Monday. They will serve as the focus for the June 14 Integration Workshop. A college-wide information and discussion session will be held Monday June 17 from 10:00 - noon to update all on the events of the workshop and progress to date.
