Announcements - May 7, 2002

Strategic Planning Open Forum: Friday May 10 from 11 - noon in 03-112; all interested staff and faculty are invited to attend to get an update report on the Foundation Workshop (May 3 and 4). The strategic planning team has been divided into task forces and co-chairs of the various task forces will report and answer questions. In addition, the work products from the first workshop and other materials related to the strategic planning partnership will be available on http://ocob-faculty.calpoly.edu/. The next workshop is June 14.

Commencement: Just a reminder to all tenure track faculty that college policy has been and still is that all t/t faculty are to attend at least one commencement ceremony an academic year (December graduation, June graduation or MBA hooding ceremony). If you didn't attend in December and/or just want to continue to be supportive of our students, graduation is June 15 at 3:00 in Mustang Stadium and the MBA hooding ceremony is June 16 at 10:00 at the PAC. Please let your ACs know which ceremonies you will be attending. See you there.

Lee Burgunder has stepped forward to once again coordinate our efforts. All those interested in participating should contact Lee.
