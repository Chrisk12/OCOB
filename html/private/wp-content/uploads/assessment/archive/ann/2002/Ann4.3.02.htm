Baker Forum - The inaugural Baker Forum is this week with a free, public session in the Cal Poly Theater on Friday, 4:30-6:00. The event willfeature Susan Hackwood, the Exec. Director of the California Council for Science and Technology, and the initial release of CCST's new report on science and technology education in CA and the implications of such for the State and nation's economic prosperity, social stability and security. Also, Cal Poly will be awarding an honorary doctorate to Walter Massey, the president of Morehouse and former director of both the National Science Foundation and the Argonne National Laboratory. This event is the capstone event of the University's Centennial year celebration. PLEASE attend this event. The college needs a strong showing.
