Logos for Downloading

To view the logo, left-click on the underlined link. To download logos, right click on the link and "save target" to your desktop.

Size of the OCOB logo can be changed by clicking on the logo within a document, pressing the "shift" key, grabbing a corner of the logo and bringing it to the desired size. You must use the "shift" key when resizing the logo. This will keep the relative dimensions of the art consistent with other uses of this OCOB trademark. "Squeezed" or "elongated" logos must not be used in any document or presentation.

Additional logos in black and gray scale are available through OCOB Multi-media Services.
