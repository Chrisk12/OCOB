Professor Tolin joined the Orfalea College of Business with more than 35 years of experience as a tax adviser, specializing in international taxation and mergers and acquisitions.  While working with a broad spectrum of clients, his focus was on clients in the entertainment industry.  His clients included The Walt Disney Company, Dreamworks SKG, Dreamworks Animation, Village Roadshow, K B Homes, Deckers, and K-Swiss.   Professor Tolin was the first National Director of Entertainment Tax Services at Ernst & Young.

Professor Tolin is a frequent speaker at various industry and tax forums which have included the USC Federal Tax Institute, California Society of CPAâ€™s, Beverly Hills Bar Association, Los Angeles Bar Association, Directors Guild of America, American Film Marketing Association, and others.  In addition, Professor Tolin has given speeches at the Cannes Film Festival on global tax incentives for the film industry.   Professor Tolin has also presented two White Papers to the Treasury Department, the National Office of the Internal Revenue Service, and to representatives of the House Ways & Means Committee in Washington, DC.

Professor Tolin has published numerous articles on taxation in various journals including the Journal of Taxation, the Journal of Multi-State Taxation, California Tax Lawyer and Tax Notes.  In addition, Professor Tolin co-authored the first tax comic book and the Ernst & Young Guide to International Film Production.
