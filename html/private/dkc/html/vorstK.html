Dr. Vorst serves as an Assistant Professor and Principal of Cal Pack Labs, Department of Industrial Technology, Plastics and Packaging Department at California Polytechnic State University in San Luis Obispo, California.  Dr. Vorst directs the technical development, design and biodegradable component of projects at Cal Poly.  Dr. Vorst has worked in industry for three major packaging companies and a consultant for a major automotive manufacturer prior to his current position at California Polytechnic State University.

Dr.Vorst published research on Listeria spp. contamination of food while at Michigan State. He works with food packaging processes to improve food safety and quality. He has also developed devices and materials for the medical community and oral airway devices and has three current or pending patents.

Dr. Vorst has several peer-review publications for polymer testing and design in the American Society for Testing of Materials (ASTM) Journal of Testing and Evaluation, Journal of Applied Polymer Science and Journal of Plastic Film and Sheeting and currently serves on the ASTM board for D10 testing of plastics.  Dr. Vorst is also serves as a reviewer for Journal of Applied Polymer Science, Journal of Food Protection, and Journal of Testing and Evaluation.

Dr Vorst has amassed over $1.8 million in research grants at Cal Poly since August 2008. He is regularly asked to provide expert advice on plastic uses and food safety issues, and recently in carbon nanotube  research. He has 13 refereed publications. He has received national and international recognition for this research.

Dr. Vorst is a founding member of the Cal Poly Research Consortium, an  Industry-led cooperative endeavor that joins Cal Poly faculty to their industry counterparts. This consortium has provided  $180-225K annual funding for graduate student support.  Dr. Vorst is also a founding member of Echo-Pac Inc. (West Lafayette, IN), a consulting firm which serves as an industry partner for food product design and safety services for court litigation and expert counsel.
