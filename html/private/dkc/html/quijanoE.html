Mr. Quijano has functioned in various positions with the Orfalea College of Business since 2003, including service as an Associate Professor; Chair, Accounting and Law Area; and Director, MSA–Taxation Program. He presently serves as Clinic Director, Cal Poly Low Income Taxpayer Clinic, Lecturer in Taxation, and Director of Advancement.

Prior to his association with Cal Poly, Mr. Quijano served as a senior trial attorney with the Office of Chief Counsel, Internal Revenue Service, in Los Angeles (1971-1975) and New Orleans (1975-1979).

In December 1979 Mr. Quijano entered the private practice of law with the firm of ADAMS AND REESE, LLP where he maintained a federal and state tax practice and business and corporate law practice from 1979 until his retirement in 2003. He served as the firm’s managing partner from 1996-2001.

Mr. Quijano was a Board Certified Tax Attorney in Louisiana from 1985 until his retirement and re-location to California in 2003. He maintains the highest Martindale-Hubbell lawyer rating (AV), and was listed in Best Lawyers in America (Taxation) from 2001-2004.

During the past 25 years, Mr. Quijano has actively participated in a wide range of charitable, civic, and community activities, particularly with the American National Red Cross.
