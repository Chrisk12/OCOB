EDUCATION:

Larry Gorman (Ph.D. finance, Northwestern U (Kellogg), 1998) is a professor of finance at Cal Poly, and a member of the Investment Oversight Committee where he helps to manage Cal Poly’s ~$150 million endowment. An award winning researcher for his stock market and exotic investment analysis, he is a sought after speaker at investment conferences. The winner of numerous teaching awards, he is the primary finance trainer worldwide for Apple, and a visiting professor each summer at the Sasin School in Bangkok, where he teaches corporate and international finance to executives and MBA’s. He sits on the board of directors of Space Wealth, which promotes the profitable mining of near earth asteroids. Prior to his finance endeavors, he worked for ABB as a field engineer in electrical power production, and as a commercial fisherman for nine seasons in Alaska’s Bearing Sea.
