Dr. Cerfis an accounting Professor and Area Chair of the accounting area. Prior to this appointment, Dr. Cerf served as the associate dean of the Orfalea College of Business responsible for undergraduate programs for six years. Dr. Cerf teaches intermediate and advanced accounting courses. Dr. Cerf has been a visiting professor at the following universities; Bren Graduate School of Environmental Science and Management at the University of California, Santa Barbara; Copenhagen Business School and at Politecnico di Milano.
