Announcements - September 18, 2002

Note these announcements were made at the college-wide meeting on September 17, but in case you missed the meeting or dozed off, they are repeated here:

Vivian Rodriguez has accepted the position as the Advancement Director for the OCOB. She'll be here on September 26 and then take some time off to get her family ready to move to the Central Coast.
