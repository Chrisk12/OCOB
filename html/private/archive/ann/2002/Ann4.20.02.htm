Your support needed: The Cal Poly Chapter of the Wheelchair Foundation is staging a fundraising event, The Gift of Mobility Benefit 2002, on Sunday, 28 April. A brunch and auction will be held at the PAC 9:30 - noon, followed by a wine tour 12:30-4:00. The goal of the event is to raise $36,000, which will pay for 240 wheelchairs (a container load) that will be distributed to needy residents in Peru. Tickets for the auction/brunch only are $45. Tickets for the full day's events (auction/brunch + wine tour) are $75. The Wheelchair Foundation is a non-profit organization founded by the Behring family. The Foundation leads an international effort to deliver a wheelchair to every person in the world who needs one. A flyer for the event has been placed in your box. For more info, go to http://www.cob.calpoly.edu/hot/Wheelchair/index.html or contact Lynn
Metcalf at lmetcalf@calpoly.edu or 756.2010.
