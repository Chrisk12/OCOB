Announcements - March 4, 2002

Welcome to the newest communications vehicle in the college.
Announcements will be sent out in electronic form on a regular basis.
The goal is to keep all informed on what's going on in the college.

Doug Cerf has agreed to continue in his role as Associate Dean.

Bill Pendergast is a faculty member on special assignment for the
college and the university working on initiatives related to
entreprenuership and community development. His office is 03-406. This
assignment currently runs through August.

FAR reports are due from the faculty on April 8, the first Monday in the
second week of Spring quarter. After April 8, all faculty FAR reports
will be available for review in the respective area offices or in the
Dean's Office. Last year's report form is updated and will be available
tomorrow at http://buiznt.cob.calpoly.edu/cob/biz (and yes - we are
checking into getting a domain name to replace this). However, no need
to wait, just make the appropriate changes in dates and content on last
year's report and you are good to go. So as to not impact Spring break
activities, as well as the first week of the quarter rush, the deadline
for FAR submissions is being announced now. (For you procrastinators,
I'm sorry I can't change your work style, so the last statement does not
apply to you.) Yes - there is a task force currently reviewing the FAR
document with the goal to provide suggested improvements to the document
and process. The task force's recommendations will go to the faculty
for their acceptance or rejection, as prescribed in the document. Given
the desire to provide ample notice on the due date of the report, and
the process required to change our current document, the leadership team
decided to move forward using our existing document.

KUDOS is another new electronic communications vehicle being implemented
by the college. Kudo \'k(y)üd- \ n. pl kudos 1: Award, Honor 2:
Compliment, Praise. Kudos will focus on sharing the news about good
things happening in the college. Again, the goal is to communicate the
good news. Please send your information to Terri. (See the first
installment of Kudos to be posted March 4.)

Meetings: It is my desire to meet with each of the areas before the end
of Winter quarter. In addition, I intend to sit down and chat with all
full time faculty and staff one-on-one before July 1. (I haven't done
the math yet so maybe you will determine it is impossible but that is my
goal.) After I finish with the full-time people, I will meet
individually with the part time group. So - ACs will be contacted
shortly regarding area meetings. Individual meetings will begin in
earnest after finals week. If anyone is going to be around during break
and wants to be one of the early ones, please let me know.

Open Door: Things are currently a bit hectic for me as I finish teaching
this quarter. However, I want to hear your ideas and thoughts - now or
later. Feel free to stop me in the hall, the mail room, the office,
etc. or send me an e-mail.

Strategic Planning Program: Ernst and Young foundation has agreed to an
in-kind gift valued at $97,000. This gift will be in the form of its
nationally recognized SPP (strategic planning program) for business
schools. A special thanks goes to Tad and Doug for their most recent
efforts in securing this grant. (See Kudos for more)

Tentative Contract: There is a tentative agreement between the CFA and
the CSU. It appears that once all parties ratify there will be a 2% GSI
for faculty effective April 1 with another 2% GSI effective July 1
(regardless of funding!). In addition there will be a 2.65% SSI for
eligible faculty effective June 30. Obviously these are just some of
the highlights.
