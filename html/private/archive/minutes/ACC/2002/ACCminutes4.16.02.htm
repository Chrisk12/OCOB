Area Chairs Council
Meeting Notes - April 16, 2002


Present: Fred Abitia, Norm Borin, Doug Cerf, Barry Floyd, Tad Miller, Ken Riener, Jim Sena, Alden Shiers, Terri Swartz


I. Approval of Minutes:

  • Minutes of April 2, 9 and 11 ACC meetings were approved as presented

2. Announcements

  • University Summer Grant information was just received by the Dean's Office. Area chairs were requested to distribute it to untenured faculty as soon as possible as deadline for submittal is May 13. These grants provide summer support for untenured faculty.
  • Open House: Friday's event will be held on the "3 acre lawn" by Room 213. Concentration and degree program sessions will be held in the classrooms on the first floor and 213. Academic areas hosting tables on Saturday will be in the Breezeway and Industrial Technology will host an Open Lab in 21-132-135

3. Recruiting:

  • A list of adjunct faculty who have taught two quarters a year for each of the past six years will be compiled. Once the contract is ratified by the Trustees in May, faculty in this classification will have priority for temporary positions next year and will be give three-year contracts.

4. OCOB Advisory Council - May 16 and 17

  • Advisory members have been invited to teach or participate in classes on May 16th from 2:00-4:00 p.m.
  • From 4:00-5:00 p.m. a panel session for MBA students will be held. The topic is "Critical Management Skills for the 21st Century."
  • At 5:00-6:00 p.m. a reception at the Alumni House will be held with dinner to follow. Area chairs were requested to extend invitations to tenure track. Efforts will be made to accommodate all interested faculty, although space is limited.

5. Area Names

  • Two areas presented name changes for their areas. After discussion, based on input from the Council, the issue was tabled until the strategic plan is complete.

6. FAR Process Schedule

  • April 8 FARs submitted to Area Chairs
  • April 18-22 FAR reviews by Area Chairs are presented to faculty
  • April 29 FAR appeals from faculty submitted due to ACC
  • April 29-May 1 FAR review by ACCs
  • May 8 FAR reviews from ACC given to faculty
  • May 15 FAR appeals from faculty due to ACC
  • May 21 ACCs review FAR appeals

7. Senior Project and Independent Study Course Proposal

  • A proposal on how to count senior projects and independent study courses was submitted. After discussion and revisions it will be presented at a future ACC meeting.

8. Courses Evaluation

A process for administering course evaluations was discussed and reviewed. This process will be finalized via email before the next ACC meeting.
