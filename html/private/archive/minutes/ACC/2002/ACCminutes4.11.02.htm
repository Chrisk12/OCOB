Present: Fred Abitia, Norm Borin, Doug Cerf, Barry Floyd, Tad Miller, Ken Riener, Jim Sena, Alden Shiers, Terri Swartz


I. Announcements:

  • Accountability Study - Send to the Provost's Office as soon as possible. List specific achievements that your areas have accomplished along with the names of the journals pertinent to area. The ARPT and FAR documents will be sent by the Dean's Office.

II. Classroom Needs

  • Doug explained the method that was used to calculate current capacity and demand based on information each area submitted.
  • A total of 488 sections for 2002-03 were identified courses to be taught. Only 423 sections were taught this year.
  • Some areas anticipated new courses in response to student requests, additional sections and/or smaller class sizes.
  • GSB courses should be counted with undergraduate courses in the appropriate area.
  • One additional large room (65 capacity) would be needed.
  • Two additional rooms in the medium category (40 capacity) would be needed if all sections are offered.
