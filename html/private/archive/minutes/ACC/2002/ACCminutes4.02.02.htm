3. Classroom Needs

  • Rush Hill is reviewing classrooms in Building 02 and 03. There is a possibility that some rooms in 02 could be remodeled to meet the needs of the OCOB.
  • Any changes in classrooms must result overall in the same number of seating capacity or an increase in seating capacity.
  • If fixed seats are removed in 03-205, an additional six or seven seats could be added. Consideration could be given to splitting 03-201 and 204 depending on the needs of courses and what makes a better classroom.
  • The areas need to define what is the optimal classroom size for each of their courses, given resource constraints.
  • Area chairs were requested to (1) list classes that fit into classrooms using categories of 25, 40 and 65 seating capacity (2) number of sections for each course offering this year and (3) project course offerings for next year-thinking of classroom utilization, i.e., change hours of classes when necessary and four-hour
