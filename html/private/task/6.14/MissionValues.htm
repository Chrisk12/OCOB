California Polytechnic State University
Orfalea College of Business
 

Mission

 

The OCOB challenges highly motivated students through technically-based business education to become tomorrow’s socially responsible business leaders.

 

Shared Values

We value:

•         Honesty and integrity

•         Openness and cooperation (versus competitiveness)

•         Mutual respect and collegiality

•         Students as people  (versus raw material)

•         Quality teaching  …   (should we instead value learning?)

•         Flexible thinking and innovation

 

Amplification of strategic concepts/phrases:

           

challenges highly motivated students

  • Committed to rigorous, relevant courses taught by dedicated, professionally active faculty
  • Committed to students actively involved in their education
  • Committed to education occurring both inside and outside the classroom
  • Committed to significant faculty student interaction

 

through technically-based business education

  • Emphasizes an understanding of the influence and uses of technology in business
  • Emphasizes technology-based business decision making  (is this redundant with previous point?)
  • Identifies the need to forge partnerships between the college and industry
  • Focuses on applied scholarship (AACSB defined)
  • Leverages the technological competencies of other colleges at Cal Poly

 

to become tomorrow’s socially responsible business leaders

  • Emphasis on integration
  • Emphasis on working in a changing, diverse environment
  • Preparation for careers
  • Preparation for life-long learning
  • Emphasis on having a positive impact on society
