Responsibility

 

•   Create a list of action steps to be performed by ACs/supervisors, and documents describing the attributes of a mentor and best mentoring practices

(See attached files—GeneralMentoring.doc , PeerMentor.doc , AreaCoordinatorMentor.doc , ResearchTeachingMentor.doc).

 

 

•   Circulate the list of attributes of a mentor to all faculty and staff, request responses from those interested in participating in the mentoring process and maintain a list of those interested in mentoring.

 

 

•   Distribute copies of action steps and mentoring documents & potential mentors to all new hires and their supervisors.

 

 

•   Area Coordinators/Supervisors complete action steps with new hires & Dean’s office appoints peer (another untenured faculty member/newly hired staff) mentor for new hires’ first quarter.

 

 

•   For faculty hires, within 1st quarter the AC and new hire choose 1 or more mentors (more than one might be appropriate if person wanted one for teaching and one for research, etc.).

 

 

•   Mentors are evaluated and rewarded for their activities as part of the service component of the FAR process.
