D. Standard M.5:  The school’s activities must be consistent with its mission.

Accomplishment Expectation:  The new mission statement includes shared values.  The team did find evidence of shared understanding about some of these values, particularly of “learn-by-doing.”  In a few instances, most notably “openness and cooperation” and “mutual respect and collegiality,” there appears to be a need for further growth and reinforcement of these values within the College.  Introduce selected regular activities designed to encourage the shared values and describe these in the report. Make clear how further development of the desired shared values will be addressed.
