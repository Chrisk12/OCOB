C. Standard M.4:  The school must specify its relative emphasis on teaching, intellectual contributions, and service.

Accomplishment Expectation:  The team found evidence of some disagreement and/or miscommunication among the faculty and between the dean’s office and the faculty concerning the relative emphasis on teaching, intellectual contributions and service that is desired for successful achievement of tenure and promotion. While guidelines exist in official policies and documents, the application and interpretation of these guidelines, particularly those concerning teaching and research, appear to be unclear among groups of faculty and across different departments/areas.  Provide a self-diagnosis of this problem and the ways in which it has been addressed.
